About

Looking to meet new people at your favorite bar? BarMingle makes it easy to connect with others and socialize in real-time, right from your seat!

How it Works?

Each seat in participating bars has a unique QR code. Scan it with BarMingle to view who's around and start a chat!

  • Discover and connect with others in the bar
  • View bar maps and available seating
  • Send messages and even order drinks for others

Features - Manager App

Bar Selection

Choose which bar to manage from your list of assigned bars, each with dedicated screens for seamless control over each bar’s specific needs.

Bar Map Management

Upload or update the bar map layout, manage seating arrangements, and add, delete, or modify seats to reflect real-time occupancy.

QR Code Generation

Generate custom QR codes for each seat based on your specified dimensions, export them as a PDF, and send the file directly to your email.

Menu Management

Update or customize the bar’s menu items for gifting options, including item details and pricing tailored to your bar’s offerings.

Gift Approval

Monitor and approve gift requests sent between patrons to ensure a smooth and controlled service experience.

Emergency Alerts

Receive real-time emergency alerts from users in your bar to ensure prompt responses to any issues that arise.

Features - User App

Seat Association

Scan a QR code to associate yourself with a seat in the bar, making it easy to connect with others in real-time.

Bar Map Viewing

Explore the interactive bar map to see available seats and who else is present, making socializing more accessible.

Chat and Connect

View profiles of other patrons and initiate chats to engage and make new connections within the bar environment.

Send Gifts

Elevate the social experience by sending drinks or food items to other users as a gesture of connection.

Profile Customization

Personalize your profile with unique details, including a biography, to enhance your social connections.

Chat History

Access past conversations with other patrons, providing a familiar messaging experience for easy reference.

Azure Services & API Usage

Azure Table Storage

Used to store user details, manage seating arrangements, store chat history, gift data, and handle emergency notifications.

Azure Blob Storage

Used for storing bar maps, menu images, QR codes, and user profile pictures securely and accessibly.

Azure Function Apps

Serverless functions handle user registration, QR code generation, seat assignments, user interactions, PDF creation, sending PDF by email and more.

SignalR Service

Enables real-time updates for bar activities, including live chats, seat status, gift notifications, and emergency alerts to managers without requiring page refreshes.

QR Code Generation & Scanning

Facilitates seat identification, enabling users to scan QR codes, generated by the bar manager, to link themselves to a seat, enhancing interaction within the bar.

PDF Creation & Email Sending

Generates QR codes in customizable PDF formats for bar seats and sends them to the manager's email, simplifying seat management.

Google Authentication

Provides secure authentication for the Web version of BarMingle, allowing users to log in with their Google accounts.

User Avatar Initialization

Generates initials-based avatars for users without profile photos, ensuring everyone has a visual representation on the bar map (e.g., "MJ" for "Michael Jordan").
